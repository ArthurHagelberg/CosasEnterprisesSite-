THE MALDIVES TOWER

The Maldives Tower is a gigantic pillar of concrete that we built to combat the rising sea levels that were once threatening the Maldives. There was one problem with this plan. space begins at roughly 100 kilometers above the earth's surface. The tower was 7,105 kilometers tall, placing the entire nation of the Maldives in outer space. In order to remedy this, we had to build a replica of the Vulcan 20-20 laser to slice off the top mile. We pushed this section off, causing it to fall a few miles away from the tower. The Maldives were habitable once again, and a mile out of the water. Problems solved.

A few months later, we began our Cosas Space Program, and we decided to build our spacecraft takeoff place on the tower. It is already in space, completely canceling out the need for an immense amount of fuel to exit the atmosphere.

Recently we also constructed a gigantic elevator that is 2 aquare miles in area. It cost $658,654,628,609.
